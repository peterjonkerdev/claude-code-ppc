# Lessons

> Self-improvement loop. After any correction or mistake, capture it here so it doesn't happen again.
> Format: date, what happened, rule to prevent recurrence.
> The agent proposes entries; you approve before they're added.

## 2025-01-15: Aggregated ratios instead of ratio of aggregates
**What happened:** Calculated average CPC as `AVG(cost / clicks)` instead of `SAFE_DIVIDE(SUM(cost), SUM(clicks))`. Got a wildly wrong number because low-volume days skewed the average.
**Rule:** Always aggregate numerator and denominator separately before dividing. Never average a pre-calculated ratio.
